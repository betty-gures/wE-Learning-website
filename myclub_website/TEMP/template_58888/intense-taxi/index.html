about

City-Taxi has been serving our community for over four decades. We are based in los Angeles and have a developed network of partner companies all over the US, which means we have the largest fleet of taxis in the North America. Our uniformed drivers adhere to international guidelines and hold a private-hire license. Our cars have prominent roof signs making them easily identifiable and our experienced team go the extra mile, providing excellent customer service at all times. If you are looking for an affordable taxi service, we can give you what you need.
